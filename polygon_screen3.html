National ID Verification


Please make a photo of the front and back sides of your National ID

After tapping „Make a photo” you might be asked for permissions to use your phone camera. Please note granting permissions is required for successful completion of the verification.
Photos will be taken in landscape mode. When you make a photo please follow the onscreen instruction and make sure it fits within the frame displayed on your screen.
Once capture make sure photos are legible before saving.
